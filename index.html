Who We Are

The Facet Group is an international television production and distribution company catering for TV and cable networks in Latin America, Europe and the United States.
Our varied contents not only serves broadcasters and the satellite markets but has expanded into the growing world of multimedia, into the global digital environment.
We provide advertisers with sponsorship opportunities for an array of events and products that target Spanish and Portuguese-speaking demographic audiences.
We build multiplatform Apps. With the usage of 3D real-time technology, we turn our cinematographic projects into interactive apps for smartphones, consoles and computer. Our projects fully support VR technologies such as Oculus Rift and YouTube 360.

Services

Backed by almost 20 years of experience in the Broadcasting industry, we provide full production services ; state-of-the-art technology operated by a team of highly qualified and competent multi-lingual professionals.
